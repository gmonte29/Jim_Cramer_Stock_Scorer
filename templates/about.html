What is this tool?

In Jim Cramer's Book Real Money, he discusses some high level trading strategies and factors to look at. Included is the relationship between Growth Rate and Price/Earnings of a company - if a company's growth rate is high but it's P/E is low, this is a sign of a potential buy grade stock while the opposite would be true of a sell grade stock. In general, a high price to earnings and low growth rate could mean the market is over valuing the company.

This tool attempts to implement this strategy by calculating the top and bottom 8 stocks of a given revenue range and sector.


What is the calculation?

The tool takes all the stocks within the given input parameters and applies the following calculation to return a buy/sell score.

(stock GR / group GR - 1)/2 + -(stock PE / Group PE - 1)/2

Where "group" is the average value for all the companies that are filtered based on the user input.


What data is used for this calculation?

The stock ticker information is brought in from AlphaVantage and the stock financial information is brought in from yFinance.
